Be humble.

保持谦虚。

謙虚（けんきょ）でいなさい。


You're always going to meet all sorts of people and encounter all sorts of things.

Stay away from the bad energy of those who are arrogant, prideful, aggressive or irritable.

People have their own dilemmas.

Keep your own pace.
